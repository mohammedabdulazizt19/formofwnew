FORM - Filipino Overseas Rescue Mission

What is FORM and what is it for?

FORM is a non-governmental organization (NGO) in the Philippines whose function is to help Manning Agencies with the deployment and monitoring of Overseas Filipino Workers. Skilled, non-skilled, professional, or non-professional, whatever your occupation may be, FORM is here to lend a helping hand.

FORM will help monitor OFW’s wellbeing, especially household service workers, if they are in a good working environment and if they can perform their jobs properly. Our main focus is also to ensure that workers are being paid regularly and not being maltreated or abused by their employers or other individuals within their vicinity.

We currently have a system that can be used for the first time in the country, with the assistance of YaraMay Computer Maintenance Services (YCMS), wherein it will help the Agencies and the Philippines Government to properly monitor our OFW’s abroad.

FORM is currently spearheaded by PCSO Board of Directors Member, Ramon Ike Seneres and YCMS General Manager, Ferdenand “Ibraheem” Sabado. Currently, FORM can now be utilized by the Agencies and the Government to help with the deployment of our OFW’s to different parts of the globe We aim to help both the Agencies and the Government on reducing the likelihood of maltreatment, abuse, and loss of life of our fellow Filipinos abroad.

What is FORM and why does it exist?

Filipino Overseas Rescue Mission (FORM) was created to monitor and protect our Overseas Filipino Workers (OFW) wherever they are in the world, especially in the Middle East.

FORM works with Manpower Agencies, who deploy workers to different parts of the globe, to monitor their well-being abroad.

Our organization can also be an aide to our Government, Embassies, Labor Offices and other branches of our government who are concerned with OFW's.

FORM will be the first ever NGO to monitor our OFW's whether they are land-based or sea-based. We created a system that can be used as legal evidence which includes monitoring data, IT support and any other means to help with the legal proceedings, to the best of the organization's ability.

FORM also offers special services to OFW's, especially those who were deployed to the Middle East.

What are the benefits which FORM may offer OFW's?

1. Wherever an OFW is deployed, whether land-based or sea-based, they will be monitored by the system established by FORM.

2. It will monitor the legality of the OFW for deployment.

3. The OFW in the Middle East will be assisted by a translator for them and the Employer to understand one another, and also to monitor the Employer.

4. FORM created this system that has never existed in the Philippines, and this should be used in our country.

5. Protect OFW's with legal evidence. Example: salary history, sold to other employer, raped, abused, and other forms of maltreatment whether physically or job-related.

The purpose of FORM is to help and save lives even through the smallest amount of effort.

Services

Provide monitoring OFW's whose contract has ended, no contract or even undocumented individuals.

To provide evidence should there be a need for legal action.

To organize a rescue mission should the need arise.

To provide protection among OFW's, Agencies and Employers.

To provide monitoring for Employers.

To alarm or enable the Philippine Government to be notified if problems arise.

To provide information and records which can be used legally or any other purpose they may be served.

Other Services

Legal advice for both labor and non-labor related problems.

Translators for OFW's, Employers and Government.
